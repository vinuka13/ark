WHAT ACTUALY IS ARK?

Ark is the name we gave our “project” to help animals, mostly dog and cats, who are in need

WHAT DO WE PLAN TO DO?

So the main idea behind Ark is to establish a shelter for stray dogs and cats in and around Kandy one day. But till we establish ourselves and find funding to build this shelter, we thought we’d start small. We are currently involved in the rescue of stray cats and dogs, in treating stray cats and dogs and also rehoming them. We operate mostly in and around Kandy, but we have reached out to other regions in the country multiple times. Now, we have been wanting to expand a little more so we can help more and more stray cats and dogs. We are thinking of little animal clinics for stray dogs, collecting funds and giving them to local animal shelters, promoting adoption of stray dogs to name a few ideas. The aim is to steadily build Ark, to one day open up that shelter!

WHERE DO YOU COME IN?

As you would have already guessed, we can’t do this alone. We need help. The help of people who truly love animals, and want to make a positive change in their lives. Which is where you guys come in. We will be advertising all our events on this website as well as on our Facebook and Instagram accounts. We will need volunteers in some instances and kind homes for stray puppies at other. And if you can help us, and if you are interested in any of these events, all you have to do is let us know. In fact, you can start off straight away by spreading the word about The Ark!. Follow our website, and other social media accounts so that you will be updated on upcoming events etc. We need all hands we can get, on the deck to pull this off!
