About us

The Ark is an animal welfare organization currently based in Sri Lanka. we are a bunch of animal enthusiast millanials driven by the mission of building a sociaty which treat animals equelly as we ourselfs wish to be treated. Our main step achive this goal is educating people and spreding awreness. In the same time we have conducted aduption programs, steralization programs and neutering programs. We have also rescued dozens of sick, disable and vulnerable strays. We are hoping to expand our family of cynophlists and create a community large enough to build the sociaty we need.
