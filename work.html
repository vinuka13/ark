Our work

She was left on the side of the road along with 5 of her siblings when she was just about 3 weeks old. She was lucky enough to find a temporary home while we worked hard to find her the forever home she deserves. And guess what, a kind lady from colombo volunteered to adopt her, despite the distance and the cost of transport. In no time she was vaccinated and heading to her new home. The pictures on the left side is the most recent, taken a few days ago. She has found a home among a loving family of two kids. She is loved and well taken care of and nothing pleases us more than seen her living her best life after a traumatic beginning.

Lily is a mom of two, and very young herself but she was kept by a family that know nothing about treating  dog right. As you can see in the picture on the left, she was malnourished, chained and has a sever rash all over her body. Her pups were in no better condition. However, despite the traumatic start, Lily was lucky to be seen by a kind lady adopted her and we were also able to find homes for her pups. You can see how happy and healthy Lily is after a few months of good care and lots of love.  Like they say, we can't change the world but we can be the change we like to see in it!

She us lucky. An we gave her that name because she was lucky enough to survive a brutal accident. She was seen  by two university students when she was lying  with half her face torn apart. They rushed her to the vet hospital. She was a fighter and survived her injuries. We could find her a kind foster home where she stayed till she fully recovered. Lucky was then adopted by a kind family and she is happy and loved there

This is Tiny. She was abandoned along withe her brother on the side of the road about 8 years ago. She was so small that when she fell into the drain one day, she couldn't even get up. Though the beginning might have been difficult both these puppies were lucky enough to find homes before long and quite close to each other too! Tiny is now a big boy, 8 years old, and is happily living with his family.

These are just the fraction of the things we have done. Also there are much more for us to do too. Make sure to follow us on our social accouts for latest information about them
